# Mercuryo.io: 01 Evidence File

## Product snapshot

- What the product does: Mercuryo provides embedded crypto payment infrastructure for web3 businesses across on-ramp, off-ramp, branded cards, and adjacent payment use cases.
- Current buyer: Product, partnerships, revenue, or growth leaders at wallets, exchanges, and web3 apps.
- Urgent problem moment: A partner wants users to buy, sell, or spend crypto without leaving the product or assembling multiple payment vendors.
- Sales motion: Enterprise and solutions-led. Mercuryo pushes contact sales, tailored offers, and onboarding support.
- Stage: Scale-up infrastructure business with public claims of 500+ customers, 12M+ KYCd users, and a 2018 origin.

## Working summary

- Settled: Mercuryo is clearly B2B and strongest around embedded web3 payment infrastructure rather than a pure consumer story.
- Uncertain: Whether the primary narrative should lead with On-Ramp scale, broader payment-engine breadth, or Spend as the newer wedge.
- Decision to force next: Choose one buyer and one core offer that makes the multi-product portfolio feel coherent.
