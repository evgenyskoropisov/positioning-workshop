# Mercuryo.io: 04 Team Pulse Packet

Answer independently. Keep answers short. Gut reaction is better than polished language.

## Context to react to

- Candidate buyer: Product, partnerships, or growth leader at a wallet, exchange, or web3 app.
- Candidate urgent problem: Users leak out of the product during buy, sell, or spend flows, and the team does not want to stitch multiple vendors together.
- Candidate wedge: One embedded payment engine across on-ramp, off-ramp, and branded cards.
- Candidate boundary: Not a consumer exchange and not only a single on-ramp widget.

## Questions

1. Which buyer matters most for Mercuryo in the next 12 months, and why?
2. Which problem moment creates urgency fastest for that buyer?
3. What should make a buyer choose Mercuryo over Ramp, Transak, or MoonPay?
4. What can Mercuryo prove today without stretching?
5. Which market label should guide the roadmap: payment engine, web3 payment infrastructure, on/off-ramp platform, or something else?
6. What are we explicitly not trying to be?

## One-line position

Write one sentence under 30 words:

We help [WHO] achieve [WHAT] through [HOW], unlike [ALTERNATIVE].

## Friction checks

1. What sounds overstated right now?
2. What should we stop saying?
3. Which phrase feels most natural for a partner team to repeat?
