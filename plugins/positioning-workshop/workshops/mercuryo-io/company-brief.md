# Mercuryo.io Company Brief

## Product

Mercuryo is a crypto payment infrastructure company for web3 businesses. Its public product set includes embedded on-ramp, off-ramp, branded card issuance through Spend, and additional payment products for partner use cases.

## Buyer

The most likely near-term buyer is a product, partnerships, revenue, or growth leader at a wallet, exchange, or web3 app that wants embedded crypto payment flows without sending users away or stitching together multiple vendors.

## Problem

Web3 businesses lose users and speed when buy, sell, and spend flows live in disconnected products or third-party redirects. They also inherit regulatory, operational, and integration complexity if they try to assemble the stack themselves.

## Why now

Mercuryo's own story points to a shift from simple fiat on-ramp toward broader crypto payment use cases: embedded buy and sell, branded cards, and everyday payment behavior. That expands the problem from "how do users enter crypto?" to "how do web3 businesses keep payment activity inside their own experience?"

## Stage

Scale-up infrastructure company. Publicly claims 500+ web3 customers, 12M+ KYCd users, a team of 300 people, and a company history starting in 2018.

## Constraints

- Message currently spans both B2B infrastructure and consumer everyday-life language.
- Product portfolio is broad enough to create ambiguity about the real wedge.
- Some branded language like "cryptopowered" and "enterprise solutions gateway" is memorable visually but weak strategically.
