# Mercuryo.io: 05 Messaging Pack

## One-line market position

We help web3 apps keep users in-product across buy, sell, and spend through one crypto payment engine, unlike stitching together ramps, cards, and compliance vendors.

## Category sentence

Mercuryo is a web3 payment engine for wallets, exchanges, and dApps that need embedded buy, sell, and spend flows, not a consumer exchange or a single-product widget vendor.

## Primary wedge

Most competitors are chosen for one payment moment. Mercuryo is strongest when it is chosen as the embedded payment layer that keeps user activity inside the product across multiple moments.

## Homepage hero

- Headline: Keep users in your product from first buy to daily spend.
- Subheadline: Mercuryo gives wallets, exchanges, and web3 apps one embedded payment engine for buy, sell, and card experiences, so growth does not leak into third-party flows.
- Primary CTA angle: Talk to sales about the fastest path from embedded on-ramp to broader payment use cases.

## Three support bullets

- Launch embedded buy and sell flows without pushing users into someone else's product.
- Extend the journey beyond onboarding with branded card experiences for wallets and web3 apps.
- Reduce vendor sprawl by running core crypto payment moments through one partner layer.

## Sales opener

Most web3 teams do not need one more payment widget. They need one payment layer that keeps users inside their own product from first purchase to everyday spend.

## Investor description

Mercuryo is building the payment engine for web3 businesses that want embedded crypto transactions, not fragmented vendor stacks. The opportunity expands as web3 products move from acquisition-only ramps toward recurring everyday payment behavior.

## Messaging guardrails

- Say: embedded payment engine
- Say: keep users in-product
- Say: one layer across buy, sell, and spend
- Avoid: seamless enterprise solutions gateway
- Avoid: cryptopowered without context
- Avoid: consumer exchange language as the lead story
