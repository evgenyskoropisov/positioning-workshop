# Mercuryo.io: Route Recommendation

## Recommended route

- Route: Wedge-led
- Decision: Lead Mercuryo as the embedded web3 payment engine that keeps users in-product across buy, sell, and spend.
- Confidence: High

## Why this route wins

- It turns Mercuryo's broad product portfolio into one clear partner outcome instead of a list of products.
- It competes on a real buyer pain: retention and launch friction caused by redirects and vendor sprawl.
- It gives Mercuryo a more ownable position than proof-only trust language or abstract category language.

## Why other routes lost

| Route | Why not now | What would change the decision |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Proof-led | Strongly believable, but too close to generic enterprise reassurance | More quantified partner ROI that creates a category-defining trust moat |
| Category-led | Feels aligned with brand language, but quickly drifts into abstraction | Clearer market adoption of "payment engine" language plus stronger public proof of cross-product platform value |

## Objections to handle

- "Is this just another on-ramp provider?" Show the buy, sell, and spend system rather than leading with a single feature.
- "Do I need the full stack to work with Mercuryo?" Clarify that partners can start with one product and expand without changing the underlying story.
- "Is this more brand than substance?" Anchor every message in visible proof like 500+ partners, 12M+ KYCd users, and product-level capabilities.
