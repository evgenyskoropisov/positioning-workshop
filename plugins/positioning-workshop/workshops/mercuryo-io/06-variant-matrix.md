# Mercuryo.io: 06 Variant Matrix

## Locked spine

- Core position: Help web3 apps keep users in-product across buy, sell, and spend through one crypto payment engine.
- Category: Web3 payment engine.
- Primary wedge: One embedded payment layer across on-ramp, off-ramp, and branded card experiences.
- Strongest proof: 500+ leading web3 companies and 12M+ KYCd users.
- Boundary: Not a consumer exchange and not only a single-widget vendor.

## Notes

- Best default variant: Homepage hero plus wallet sales deck.
- Highest-converting hypothesis: Wallet sales deck because Mercuryo's card and embedded-flow story feels most concrete there.
- Variant most at risk of drift: Investor narrative because it can slide back into abstract infrastructure language.
- Claims that need tighter proof: Any claim about market-leading compliance, unified platform economics, or measurable retention lift across all products.
