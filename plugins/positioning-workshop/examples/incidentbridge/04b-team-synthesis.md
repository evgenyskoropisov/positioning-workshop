# IncidentBridge: 04b Team Synthesis

## Strong agreement

- The primary buyer is the Head of Platform or an engineering leader who owns reliability outcomes.
- The most acute pain is not alerting; it is reconstructing causality after the alert fires.
- "Causal timeline" is the phrase people repeat naturally.
- The product should not be pitched as a replacement for observability tooling.

## Hidden disagreements

- Some teammates want a broader "incident intelligence" story, while others want a narrower investigation layer.
- Some want to lead with AI assistance, while others think that hurts credibility at this stage.
- There is mild disagreement about whether product engineering managers belong in the primary audience or should remain secondary.

## Unsupported claims

- Autonomous root cause analysis.
- Complete source of truth for reliability.
- Universal coverage across all engineering tools.

## Useful language

- "Build the causal timeline faster"
- "Bridge product and platform signals during incidents"
- "Reduce investigation thrash after the alert"

## Decision

Position IncidentBridge as an incident investigation layer with a clear wedge against dashboard-first observability plus manual coordination. Keep the broader category story for investor conversations, not the default homepage narrative.
