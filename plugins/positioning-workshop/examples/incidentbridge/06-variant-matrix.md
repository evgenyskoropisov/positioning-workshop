# IncidentBridge: 06 Variant Matrix

## Locked spine

- Core position: Help product and platform teams cut incident investigation time through causal cross-tool timelines.
- Category: Incident investigation layer.
- Primary wedge: Reconstructs what changed across product and infrastructure systems after alerts fire.
- Strongest proof: 42 percent median reduction in investigation time across 3 paid pilots.
- Boundary: Not a full observability suite and not an incident orchestration tool.

## Notes

- Best default variant: Homepage plus platform leader outbound.
- Highest-converting hypothesis: Platform leader outbound because the pain is sharp and measurable.
- Variant most at risk of drift: Investor deck because it invites broader category language.
- Claims that need tighter proof: Any statement about autonomous diagnosis or enterprise-wide standardization.
