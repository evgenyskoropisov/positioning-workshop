# IncidentBridge: 05 Messaging Pack

## One-line market position

We help product and platform teams cut incident investigation time through causal cross-tool timelines, unlike dashboard-first observability stacks.

## Category sentence

IncidentBridge is an incident investigation layer for software teams that need causal context after alerts fire, not another broad observability suite.

## Primary wedge

Existing tools show health, alerts, and process. IncidentBridge reconstructs what changed across product and infrastructure systems so teams can test the right hypothesis sooner.
