# IncidentBridge Company Brief

## Product

IncidentBridge helps product and platform teams investigate software incidents faster by building a causal timeline across deploys, feature flags, logs, support tickets, and on-call actions.

## Buyer

Primary buyer is the Head of Platform or an engineering leader who owns reliability for a B2B SaaS product.

## Problem

Once an incident starts, teams lose time stitching together evidence across too many tools. The alert arrives quickly enough, but understanding what changed, what broke, and what to test next is slow and messy.

## Why now

Teams ship more often, feature flags are everywhere, and product-side changes now break customer workflows as often as infra failures do.

## Stage

Seed-stage company with founder-led sales.

## Traction

- 9 active design partners
- 3 paying pilots
- median investigation time reduced by 42 percent in paid pilots
- strongest adoption in B2B SaaS teams with 50 to 300 engineers

## Constraints

- not a full observability suite
- not a replacement for incident response tooling
- limited integrations compared with established platforms
