# IncidentBridge: Route Recommendation

## Recommended route

- Route: Wedge-led
- Decision: Make the causal cross-tool timeline the center of the story.
- Confidence: High

## Why this route wins

- It gives IncidentBridge a clear reason to exist beside observability and incident tooling.
- It matches the pain buyers already feel during live incidents.
- It stays ambitious enough to be memorable without claiming platform replacement.

## Why other routes lost

| Route | Why not now | What would change the decision |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Proof-led | Credible but less differentiated on first touch | More benchmark data that turns speed alone into a category-defining proof point |
| Category-led | Too broad for current proof and product scope | Stronger market pull, clearer terminology, and more evidence that buyers want a broader story |

## Objections to handle

- "Is this just another observability dashboard?" Clarify that the wedge is investigation context after alerts, not monitoring coverage.
- "Do I need to rip out current tooling?" Reinforce that the product works with existing stacks rather than replacing them.
- "Is the category too narrow?" Show that incident investigation is a painful budget-worthy bottleneck today.
