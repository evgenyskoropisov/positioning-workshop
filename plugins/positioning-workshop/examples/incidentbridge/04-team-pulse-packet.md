# IncidentBridge: 04 Team Pulse Packet

Answer independently. Keep answers short. Gut reaction is better than polished wording.

## Questions

1. Which buyer matters most in the next 12 months, and why?
2. Which incident moment creates urgency fastest for that buyer?
3. What should make a buyer choose us over dashboard-first observability tools plus manual investigation?
4. What can we prove today without stretching?
5. Which market label should guide the roadmap: incident investigation layer, incident intelligence platform, or something else?
6. What are we explicitly not trying to be?

## One-line position

Write one sentence under 30 words:

We help [WHO] achieve [WHAT] through [HOW], unlike [ALTERNATIVE].

## Friction checks

1. What sounds overstated right now?
2. What should we stop saying?
3. What phrase here feels most natural to repeat?
