# Team Pulse Packet

Answer independently. Keep answers short. Gut reaction is better than polished language.

## Questions

1. Which buyer matters most in the next 12 months, and why?
2. Which problem moment creates urgency fastest?
3. What should make a buyer choose us over the main alternative?
4. What can we prove today without stretching?
5. Which market label should guide the roadmap?
6. What are we explicitly not trying to be?

## One-line position

Write one sentence under 30 words:

We help [WHO] achieve [WHAT] through [HOW], unlike [ALTERNATIVE].

## Friction checks

1. What sounds overstated right now?
2. What should we stop saying?
3. What phrase here feels most natural to repeat?
